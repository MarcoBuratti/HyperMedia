Shipping Countries

We currently ship our books to all European Union Countries and to the United Kingdom.

When we ship

All orders are shipped out within 2 business days after the order has been placed. Once your order has been shipped out you will be notified through e-mail. This e-mail will also contain the tracking code for your order. If you have any questions regarding the status of your order please contact us. Books are usually delivered within 5 to 7 business days after they are shipped out.

Shipping Cost

The shipping cost does not depend on the number of books ordered. The fixed shipping cost is € 5,00 for shipping within EU, 6,50 £ for shipping to the UK.

Refunds & Exchanges

Your satisfaction is our top priority and we will do what we can to ensure your happiness with every purchase.
Please do not hesitate to contact us at anytime if you are unhappy with your order.
Returns are processed as store-credit/refunds or exchange. We may accommodate refunds only if product is returned due to a damage/defect.
In order to receive store-credit/refund, please reach out to us within 14 days of receiving your purchase. If a product arrives broken or damaged, we are happy to send a replacement.
However a photograph as proof of the damage/defect is required.

Order Cancellation

To cancel an order that has not yet shipped have your order number available and contact us. We are unable to process cancellations for items that have already shipped.

Payment Options

We accept all major credit cards through Paypal payment processing.
